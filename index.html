About Me

Hello! I'm Agustin Martinez, a programmer from Argentina with a passion for web development and teaching.

I have a solid foundation in web development, with hands-on experience in core front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as proficiency in frameworks like Angular for building dynamic user interfaces. My skill set extends to back-end development with Java and PHP, and I'm adept at managing databases, particularly MySQL. Beyond technical skills, my role as a teacher at UTN has been incredibly rewarding, allowing me to develop and deliver course material for subjects such as Architecture & Operating Systems, Programming, and Mathematics, effectively bridging theory and practice.

Currently, I'm advancing in my Programming degree at UTN Mar del Plata, with an expected graduation in 2025, where I'm further honing my expertise in areas like Object-Oriented Programming with Java, C, and advanced front-end and back-end frameworks. I am actively seeking opportunities to immerse myself in challenging projects where I can leverage my blend of technical acumen and proven teaching abilities. My goal is to contribute to innovative solutions, continuously expand my knowledge, and collaborate effectively within a team to achieve outstanding results.
